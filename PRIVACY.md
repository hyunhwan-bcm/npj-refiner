# Privacy Policy for npj refiner

**Last Updated: December 31, 2025**

## Overview
npj refiner is a Chrome extension that enhances the reading experience on Springer Nature journal websites by improving image quality, mathematical equation rendering, and automatically expanding tables.

## Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

## What the Extension Does
The extension operates entirely within your browser and:
- Modifies image URLs to display high-resolution versions
- Configures MathJax rendering settings (stored locally in browser cookies)
- Fetches table content from the same Nature website you're viewing to display it inline
- Makes no external network requests to any third-party servers
- Does not track your browsing activity
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not use analytics or telemetry

## Permissions
The extension requires host permissions for `*.nature.com` and `*.springernature.com` domains to:
- Access and enhance images on Nature journal pages
- Configure mathematical equation rendering
- Fetch and display full tables inline

These permissions are used solely for the extension's stated purpose and do not enable data collection.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
